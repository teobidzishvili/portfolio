About Me

Programming rolled into my life during Covid19, when I found myself at home with no business. Bitcamp got me familiar with programming fundamentals and making lockdown period interesting for me.
When I returned to my job in construction field, day by day I've been convincing that programming was what I needed in my life. Therefore, I quit all of the jobs and involved in Full-Stack Engineering program on Bitcamp, that helped me to stand on the right path that I am walking down right now.
Although I turned my career towards programming, I have left all of the information about my previous experience and education in construction and energy fields on my resume, as I have gained priceless knowledge and experience, that makes me proud and I am sure that it will be useful at any stage of my career or personal life.
Scientific work is also my field of interest. While looking for information about possibilites, Bussines and Technology University got my attention, therefore for 2023 I plan to apply for its DevOps master program.
